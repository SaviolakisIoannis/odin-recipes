Pasta fazool

Description

Pasta fazool is classic Italian American comfort food. This recipe works with any sausage, but I like the fennel and anise flavors of sweet Italian sausage with the beans. A hearty soup that's fast and easy to put together.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • 1 stalk celery, diced
  • 3 cups chicken broth, or more as needed, divided
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 3 cups chopped Swiss chard
  • 1 (15 ounce) can cannellini (white kidney) beans, drained
  • 1 cup grated Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese, plus additional for serving, or to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a skillet over medium-high heat
  2. Stir in tomato paste until evenly distributed, 2 to 3 minutes
  3. Place chopped chard in a bowl.
  4. Stir in white beans; continue cooking and stirring until pasta is tender, 4 to 5 minutes.
